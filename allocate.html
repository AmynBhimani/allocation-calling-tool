Step 1 · Run & seed

Run the allocation

Review-migration assignments stay put (including Medical & Registration & Access). IFF, no-birthday, and under-16 volunteers are held aside — except under-16s eligible for an area that explicitly admits them (Environmental, 13+). Nobody is ever placed in an area they didn't pick — the only "anywhere" people are those who chose "happy to serve anywhere." Allocation runs in two phases whose order you set with Fill order: pickers (each area topped up from its own pickers over the rounds) and happy-anywhere. The happy phase always seeds the two no-picker areas (Environmental, Memorabilia) to target first, then fills everyone else over the same number of passes. Flex priority sets where the happy people go: furthest below target sends them to whichever area is most behind its goal (so raising an area's % pulls flex straight into it), or scarcest first sends them to the areas with the fewest other candidates. With Fill all on, anyone still unplaced is then pushed into the least-subscribed area they actually selected — allowing overage — so no one is left Unassigned and you can see which areas are oversubscribed (the +over column). More rounds = smoother balancing (4 rounds = 25% slices). The seed makes the draw reproducible.

Step 2 · Target percentages

Goal for the final mix

Each percentage is the share you want in that area after allocation, per region. An area's goal count = percentage × (review-assigned + assignable people in that region). The age boxes set who's eligible for each area and are fully editable: leave min blank for the default 16 floor (or set it below 16 — e.g. 13 — to admit younger volunteers, which releases those under-16s from the Young hold for that area only), and leave max blank for no upper limit. Percentages should add up to 100%.
