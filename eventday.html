Sign out
Everyone placed into a session, by session, area and duty. Someone working both sessions appears once for each. Volunteers placed in an area but not yet given a specific duty are the floating reserve and are listed under Ever Ready Team, carrying the area they came from. Check in after keeps only people checking in strictly later than the time you set (set 06:59 to include 07:00 itself). Check-in comes from the duty roster, so a lead checks in an hour before their duty, and anyone without a duty has no check-in time and drops out whenever that filter is set.
