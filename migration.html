Step 1 · Safety

Back up the workspace first

Downloads a point-in-time JSON of every volunteer record to your own drive. Always do this before a reset.

Download backup
Step 2 · Reset

Clear the volunteer workspace

Wipes the BC / Prairies / Edmonton volunteer shards so test data can't mix with the real transfer. Events, roles, and the duty catalog are left intact. Type RESET to enable the button.

Step 3 · Import

Load volunteers from Better Impact

Run the BI import on the BI import page. People arrive with a suggested area but stay Unassigned until the review is brought across — nobody is callable yet.

Step 4 · Bring the review across

Transfer reconcile decisions

Reads the review tool directly (shared storage) and applies each person's reconciled area: a single area becomes Stable with a Didar row; two or more competing areas become In reconciliation; leaders are flagged. Preview changes nothing — check the counts first, then commit.

Step 5 · Mass-accept the team

Accept the migrated team members

The migration only brings over people selected as part of an existing team. This marks the eligible ones as Accepted without a call, exactly like a caller's Accept — they flow to the iVol report and count everywhere. The usual guards still apply: anyone already accepted, on a caller's list, still in reconciliation, with no area, or flagged leadership is left out (and shown below). Nothing is selected by default — load, review, then accept.
